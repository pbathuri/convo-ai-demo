
# 📄 Convo AI — One-Pager

## 🧠 What is Convo AI?

Convo AI is a Duolingo-style conversational simulation platform designed to help users improve professional communication across various domains. It combines emotionally-aware AI agents, voice interaction, skill tracking, and gamified learning into a single app.

## 🏗️ Core Architecture

- 💬 Persona-based conversation agents
- 🧠 Emotional analysis of user tone
- 📈 Skill progression maps with milestones
- 🎮 XP, streaks, and goal-based progression
- 🎙️ Speech-to-text & text-to-speech engine
- 🧩 Modular architecture — plug & play domains

## 🎯 Target Audience

- Business professionals
- University students
- Debate teams
- Non-native English speakers looking to improve clarity and confidence

## 🔍 Differentiators

- Realistic human-like simulation
- Dynamic scoring + coaching based on communication tone
- Fully animated Duolingo-style UI map
- Multi-agent simulation (panels, debates)

## 📊 Current Modules

- Political Science
- Business Communication
- Debate Simulation
- Emotional Intelligence
- Philosophy
- Satirical Commentary
- AI & Society
- Sales Conversations

## 💡 Vision

To become the go-to platform for immersive, emotionally aware communication practice — one conversation at a time.
